# Project 7: Google AdWords Keyword Generation for Online Furniture Retailer

## Business Case

A digital marketing agency has been approached by a massive online retailer of furniture to create large-scale AdWords campaigns. The challenge is to generate an effective keyword strategy that attracts the right customers at the right cost. The furniture retailer is a low-cost provider focused on value propositions and promotions, requiring keyword strategy aligned with budget-conscious customers rather than luxury buyers.

## Problem Statement

Develop a comprehensive keyword strategy for Google AdWords campaigns targeting furniture buyers. The strategy must:
1. Focus on keywords attracting budget-conscious, deal-seeking customers
2. Emphasize promotions, discounts, and value
3. Move away from luxury and premium keywords
4. Cover the retailer's product range
5. Maximize ROI through cost-effective targeting

## Target Product Categories

- Sofas
- Convertible Sofas
- Love Seats
- Recliners
- Sofa Beds

## Strategy Overview

### Keyword Categories

1. **Brand Keywords**
   - Branded terms and variations
   - Company name searches

2. **Product Keywords**
   - Specific furniture types (sofas, recliners, etc.)
   - Product features (convertible, bed, sectional)
   - Size and style variations

3. **Intent Keywords**
   - Purchase intent: "buy sofa online", "affordable couch"
   - Comparison: "cheap sofas", "best value sofas"
   - Problem-solution: "sofa for small space", "affordable sectional"

4. **Promotion & Value Keywords**
   - Deal-focused: "sofa deals", "furniture sale", "discount sofas"
   - Pricing: "cheap furniture", "affordable sofas", "budget sofas"
   - Urgency: "furniture clearance", "limited time offer"

5. **Long-Tail Keywords**
   - Specific combinations: "affordable convertible sofa under $500"
   - Local keywords: "cheap sofas near me", "furniture delivery [city]"
   - Question-based: "where to buy cheap sofas", "best budget furniture online"

### Keywords to AVOID

- Luxury keywords: "designer sofa", "premium furniture", "high-end"
- Expensive product keywords: "leather sectional", "custom-made"
- Brand competitors (premium): "Restoration Hardware", "West Elm" (unless negative bid)
- Generic expensive keywords: "furniture boutique", "exclusive furniture"

## Campaign Structure

### Ad Groups by Theme

1. **Affordable Sofas Group**
   - Keywords focusing on price-conscious sofa buyers
   - Ad copy emphasizing value and deals

2. **Convertible Furniture Group**
   - Space-saving solutions
   - Multi-functional furniture
   - Keywords: "convertible sofa bed", "daybed sofa"

3. **Promotions & Deals Group**
   - Seasonal promotions
   - Limited-time offers
   - Clearance keywords

4. **Local Delivery Group**
   - Location-specific keywords
   - Delivery-focused messaging

5. **Specific Product Groups**
   - Recliners
   - Love Seats
   - Sectionals

## Bid Strategy

- **High Bids:** High-intent keywords ("buy sofa online", "affordable couch deal")
- **Medium Bids:** Product category keywords ("sofas", "recliners")
- **Lower Bids:** Branded terms and awareness keywords
- **Negative Bids:** Competitor premium brands, irrelevant terms

## Performance Metrics

- Click-Through Rate (CTR)
- Cost Per Click (CPC)
- Quality Score optimization
- Conversion Rate
- Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)
- Average Order Value (AOV)

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- **Target CTR:** Industry benchmark for furniture (2-3%)
- **Target CPC:** Keep below average order value / conversion rate
- **Quality Score:** Maintain 7+ through relevant keywords and landing pages
- **Conversion Target:** 2-5% depending on campaign maturity

2. Create compelling ad copy emphasizing value
