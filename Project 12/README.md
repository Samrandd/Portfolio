# Project 12: NYC Airbnb Market Analysis

## Business Case

New York City is one of the most popular tourist destinations in the world, generating enormous demand for short-term accommodations. Airbnb has become a dominant force in the NYC hospitality market, with thousands of listings across the city's five boroughs. Understanding pricing dynamics, market trends, and competitive positioning is critical for both hosts maximizing revenue and investors analyzing market opportunities.

## Problem Statement

Analyze the NYC Airbnb market to provide insights into:
1. Pricing trends and factors influencing prices
2. Listing distribution across neighborhoods and boroughs
3. Room type preferences and market demand
4. Host performance and rating patterns
5. Market opportunities and investment recommendations

## Dataset Overview

**Source:** NYC Airbnb Market Data

**Key Attributes:**
- Listing ID and name
- Host information and reviews
- Room type (entire home, private room, shared room)
- Location (borough, neighborhood, coordinates)
- Price per night
- Minimum stay requirements
- Availability data
- Review counts and ratings
- Host response time and acceptance rate

## Analysis Areas

### 1. Pricing Analysis
- Average prices by borough and neighborhood
- Room type pricing distribution

### 3. Room Type Analysis

- Revenue potential by room type
- Average ratings by room type

### 4. Ratings and Reviews
- Average ratings by location
- Review frequency and patterns

- Guest satisfaction indicators
- Review sentiment analysis (if applicable)

### 5. Host Performance
- Super Host percentage by borough

- Response times
